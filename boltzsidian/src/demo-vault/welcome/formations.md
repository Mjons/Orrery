# Formations

Formations are named filters that isolate a subset of the universe. Press **Shift+F** to open the rail. Try **Halo** (zero-link notes), **Protostars** (last 14 days), **Solo folder**, **Galactic core**.

**Try it:** press Shift+F, then pick Halo. Notice the far-out star that lights up.
