# Your privacy

Your notes live on your disk. Nothing leaves unless you explicitly send it — the Claude API backend is opt-in, off by default. No telemetry, no phone-home, no cloud. The app writes to files and reads from files; it's a text editor that happens to have a universe.

**Try it:** press `\` to open settings. Scroll to Voice backend — everything is local until you choose otherwise.
