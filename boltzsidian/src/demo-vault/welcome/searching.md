# Searching

Press **Cmd+K** (or **Ctrl+K**, or just **/**) to open the search strip at the top. Type any word — matching stars glow, non-matches dim. ↑/↓ walks the ranked list, Enter opens, Esc closes.

**Try it:** press Cmd+K and type "linking."
