# Clusters

Stars that link densely to each other form glowing regions — clusters. You don't define them; the graph does. The meaning filter looks for collisions between notes from different clusters during [[Dreaming]]. That's often where a new idea is hiding.

**Try it:** zoom out with the scroll wheel. The glowing regions are your clusters.
