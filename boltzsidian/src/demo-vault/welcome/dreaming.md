# Dreaming

When you're idle for a while, the universe enters dream mode — physics loosens, ideas collide, and the app generates candidate new thoughts. Press **D** to read the morning report from the last cycle, or **Shift+D** to dream right now.

**Try it:** press D. A modal summarises what the universe noticed.
