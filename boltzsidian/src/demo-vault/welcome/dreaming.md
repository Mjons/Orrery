# Dreaming

When you're idle for a while, the universe enters dream mode — physics loosens, ideas collide, and the app generates candidate new thoughts. Press **Cmd+D** on demand to see the morning report: weather, three things worth noticing, prunings.

**Try it:** press Cmd+D. A modal summarises what the universe noticed.
