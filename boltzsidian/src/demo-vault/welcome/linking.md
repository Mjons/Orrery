# Linking

Connect two stars so they orbit each other. Two ways: type `[[` inside any note and pick a title from the autocomplete, or hold **Alt** and drag from one star to another in the universe.

**Try it:** press Esc, hold Alt, drag from me to any other star. A thin line appears between us.
