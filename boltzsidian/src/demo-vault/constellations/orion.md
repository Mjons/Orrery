# Orion

The anchor of the winter sky. Three belt stars, a sword hanging below
them, [[Betelgeuse]] at the red shoulder and [[Rigel]] at the blue foot.

If I can find Orion I can find everything else in winter. Drop south
from the belt to [[Sirius]]. Go up to the Hyades and the [[M45
Pleiades|Pleiades]]. In the sword, [[M42 Orion Nebula|M42]] — the
naked-eye nebula, the obvious one everyone sees first.

The whole constellation sits inside the Orion Molecular Cloud Complex,
a shared star-forming region only about 1,300 light-years away. Betelgeuse
is actually the odd star out — the other bright stars in Orion are all
around the same distance, born from the same gas.
