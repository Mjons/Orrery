# Rigel

The blue-white foot of [[Orion]] — usually the constellation's brightest
star, trading places with [[Betelgeuse]] during the Great Dimming but
otherwise holding first place.

A blue supergiant about 860 light-years away; it's the same kind of star
as [[Deneb]] but closer and bluer. It has at least three companions, two
of which (Rigel B and C) you can separate in an 8" scope on a good night.

Rigel's luminosity is something like 120,000 solar — the same neighbourhood
as Deneb. Blue supergiants are brief: at that burn rate, Rigel has only
about eight million years total to live, and we're catching it toward
the end.

Related: [[Supernova]].
