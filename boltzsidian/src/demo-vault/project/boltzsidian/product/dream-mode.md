# Dream mode

The load-bearing differentiator. When idle, the app descends into a
dream regime: physics loosens, camera auto-orbits, chorus slows but
keeps talking, and everything gets logged. On wake, you get a
[[Morning report]] — date range, peak depth, three captions from the
cycle, and a list of orphan notes worth considering.

Single slider: **Sleep Depth**, 0..1. Every parameter in the sim is a
linear blend between wake and dream profiles keyed on that number.

The payoff sentence: [[Three things before coffee]]. The risk:
[[Dream becoming mystical]].

Landed as [[Phase 5 — dream mode]]. [[Meaning filter]] is the
follow-up that turns dream-produced junk into occasional keepers.
