# First-run experience

What happens in the user's first five minutes. Load-bearing craft
surface — we want the app to teach itself.

What we ship:

- Welcome card with two options: open your own folder, or try a
  curated demo vault (installed into [[OPFS sandbox]]).
- Tag discovery modal if the configured tag→kind map covers <80% of
  the user's notes.
- Coachmarks — one-shot tips that fire on the first time the user
  performs each gesture (click a star, press `N`, alt-drag, right-click).
- About pane reachable from the welcome card and Settings.

Built as [[Phase 3.5 — first run]].
