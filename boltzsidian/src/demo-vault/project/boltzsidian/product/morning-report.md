# Morning report

Modal shown on wake from a dream, or on `D` any time (canned-template
preview before real dream artifacts exist).

Sections:

- **Header** — date range of the dream, peak depth, or just today's date
  if there's no dream yet.
- **Weather** — a single line of counts.
- **Three things** — top 3 distinct captions from the cycle.
- **Prunings worth noticing** — orphans old enough to suggest review.
- **Actions** — Load full dream, Discard, Close.

The full dream log lives at `.universe/dreams/YYYY-MM-DD.md`. Multiple
dreams in one day append as separate sections under the same file.

The promise: [[Three things before coffee]]. Don't let this become a
fortune-teller ([[Anti-mysticism]]).

Related: [[Dream mode]], [[Observer chorus]].
