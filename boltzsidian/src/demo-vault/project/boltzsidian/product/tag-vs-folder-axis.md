# Tag vs. folder axis

Two axes of note metadata, two different visual channels:

- **Tags** drive _kind_ — the body's colour, size, pulse. A `#person`
  is a different kind of thought than a `#decision`, so the body looks
  different.
- **Folders** drive _region_ — an optional soft halo around the body
  tinted by top-level folder. Folders don't say what _kind_ of thought
  this is; they say what area of life it belongs to.

Asymmetry: tags touch the body, folders touch the space around it.

The folder basin force (in [[Formations]]) is off by default. Most
vaults dissolve into pure link-graph positioning. For users who want
folders as spatial metaphor, the slider goes up.
