# Observer chorus

Ambient voice layer. Every ~10 s (density-configurable) the app picks
a note, looks at its neighbourhood, and floats a sentence nearby —
"{title} is quiet tonight" or "A cluster around {tag} has formed."

Sentences come from a template library; slots are filled strictly from
vault data (title, tag, folder, age, neighbour-title). Nothing is
invented. See [[Anti-mysticism]] for the tonal rule.

Off by default. On, the universe feels lived-in. Off, it's just a
graph with nice physics.

Landed as [[Phase 4 — observer chorus]]. Risk list:
[[Chorus becoming slop]].

Related: [[Morning report]] (consumes the chorus buffer),
[[Dream mode]] (chorus fires during a dream regardless of user toggle).
