# The flat universe ideal

Notes are nodes in one space. No tree. No sidebar. No "where does this
live." Every note is one `Cmd/Ctrl+K` away from any other note.

The whole premise follows from this: if there's no hierarchy to hide
behind, links and tags have to carry the structural weight. That's what
the universe metaphor rewards: connections become physical, so you
_feel_ whether a note is held in place or drifting.
