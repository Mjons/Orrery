# Three things before coffee

> "We are building this so that every morning, before coffee, the
> universe has three things to tell you about your own notes."

The one sentence the whole project hangs off. When a feature doesn't
serve this sentence, it's out of scope.

Three is the hard cap. More than three is noise. Less than three is
the system admitting it found nothing, which is also fine — the user
sees the quiet morning and knows.

The three things are **candidates, not insights**. See
[[Anti-mysticism]] for why that matters. The morning report modal
([[Morning report]]) is the artifact this promise produces.

Related: [[The flat universe ideal]], [[Dream mode]].
