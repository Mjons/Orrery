# Tufte

Edward Tufte. Data ink, small multiples, "above all else show the
data." Relevant to this project specifically in the discipline around
[[Glass aesthetic]] and [[One accent]] — decorate nothing, let the
content read.

Also useful as a corrective to the temptation to add a second accent
or gradient. "I put chartjunk into my design" is always a tempting
move. Tufte's voice in the back of the head stops it.

Related: [[Anti-mysticism]] — both are arguing against theater.
