# Andy Matuschak

Thinker-in-public on spaced repetition, note-taking, and tools for
thought. His writing on evergreen notes and the argument that "notes
should surprise you" is one of the precursors to this project's
anti-mysticism posture — insight has to come from the substrate, not
from the tool posing as oracle.

Useful reference for the [[Meaning filter]] design: Matuschak's notion
of a note earning its status by being linked to repeatedly is almost
the opposite framing of what [[Dream mode]] does — which is arguable
and interesting.

Related: [[Tufte]], [[Obsidian (the app)]].
