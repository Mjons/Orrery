# One accent

Single accent colour throughout. No second accent. No complementary
hue for "success" or "warning" or "active." Everything communicative
uses either the accent or a desaturated grey.

Default is the sim's `#8ab4ff` — a cool blue that survives bloom
without going cyan. User-editable in Settings → Appearance.

This sounds restrictive and is. It's also the single cheapest way to
keep the app from looking like a generic Vite starter. Glass + one
accent + good typography covers 80% of the aesthetic effort.

Related: [[Glass aesthetic]], [[The flat universe ideal]].
