# Collab rooms

Multiplayer editing — a webtoon lead and their assistants in the
same page at the same time. Figma-style presence cursors, locking
per panel, comments.

Technically doable on [[Supabase backend]] realtime channels.
Product-wise a very different app. Moderation, permissions, billing
for shared seats, pricing changes.

Decision: not in 1.0, not in 1.1. Reconsider at 2.0 if the
[[Webtoon lead]] personas keep asking. They haven't yet — they
mostly do hand-off, not real-time collab.

Related: [[Webtoon lead]], [[Pro tier]].
