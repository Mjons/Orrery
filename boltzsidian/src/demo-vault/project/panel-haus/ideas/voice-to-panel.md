# Voice to panel

Half-baked idea. Creator describes a scene out loud ("okay, it's a
cafe, two characters, the taller one is leaving, three panels");
we transcribe and hand the result to [[AI panel layout]].

Useful for storyboard artists ([[Storyboard artist]]) who are
sketching on paper and want to narrate rather than type. Useful for
accessibility.

Cost: every minute of voice → Whisper → transcription → layout-model
→ layout. Latency budget 3–4 seconds. Feasible.

Cut from 1.0 scope. Keep the note alive for 1.1.

Related: [[AI panel layout]].
