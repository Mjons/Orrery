# Export formats

Pro-tier differentiator. Covers every platform a working comic artist
actually posts to:

- **Webtoon vertical** — long single-column, specific guide rails.
- **Manga print** — 600 DPI CMYK with bleed + safe zones.
- **Page PDF** — for script-and-page submission.
- **Panel-strip** — horizontal social-media cuts.
- **PSD bundle** — per-panel layers for users who finish in
  Photoshop / Clip Studio.

The PSD path is the one people pay for. Takes a Canvas render and
repacks as a layered PSD using `ag-psd`. Painful, niche, worth it.
