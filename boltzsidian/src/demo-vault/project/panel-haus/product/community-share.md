# Community share

Optional per-page "share" button that publishes a read-only view to
`panelhaus.app/~user/slug`. Works on the free tier.

Social gravity: creators post their works-in-progress, followers
comment, the Pro upgrade becomes "I want to show this better."

Deliberately not a full social network. No DMs, no feed algorithm,
no notifications. Just a shareable link and a thin comments thread.

Open question: do we allow remixing (fork this layout)? Great for
learners, could be terrible for IP. Leaning toward "no, with an
opt-in toggle later."
