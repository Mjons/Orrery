# AI content backlash

Comics and manga communities are vocal about AI-generated art.
Justified — there's active career damage happening. An "AI comics
tool" walks into a room that is already on fire.

Our posture: the AI is a _layout collaborator_, not an art generator.
See [[AI panel layout]]. The artist draws. The app sequences.

Rules we won't cross:

- No full-page AI art generation.
- No "style transfer" from other artists' work.
- [[Stability API]] reference sketches are gated behind explicit
  opt-in each time, labelled "reference only," and watermarked.

If we get big enough that "Panel Haus uses AI" becomes a bad-faith
headline, the response is to point at this note.
