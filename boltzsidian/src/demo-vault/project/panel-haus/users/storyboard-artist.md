# Storyboard artist

Not a comics creator — a film / animation storyboard artist. Uses
the panel editor for shot sequencing, timing notes, camera
annotations on top of quick thumbnails.

Smaller audience than comics creators but they pay faster and
upgrade to Pro sooner. They have production budgets.

What they need that's different:

- Per-panel duration field.
- Shot type / camera move annotations.
- Export as animated pitch PDF or a rough "animatic" mp4.
- Clean integration with [[Export formats]] — they send the PDF
  directly to directors.

Might split into its own sub-product post-1.0 if the overlap with
comics creators stays low.

Related: [[Export formats]], [[Pro tier]].
