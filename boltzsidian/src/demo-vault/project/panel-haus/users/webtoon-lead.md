# Webtoon lead

Semi-pro or pro creator leading a serialised webtoon. Weekly cadence,
a team of assistants, a publishing platform they answer to.

What they need:

- Long vertical layout optimised for mobile reading ([[Export formats]]).
- Character / asset library across chapters ([[Cloud library]]).
- Co-editing or at least export-handoff to assistants.
- [[Webtoons partner]]-level export presets that match the platform's
  specs exactly.

They are the conversion target for upper-tier Pro plans. They also
talk to each other — two happy webtoon leads is our cheapest growth
channel.
