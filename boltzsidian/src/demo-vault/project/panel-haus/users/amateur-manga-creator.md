# Amateur manga creator

The persona the free tier is aimed at. Draws panels for fun, posts
on socials, reads tutorials, wants to stop fighting layout and just
_draw_.

What they need:

- Fast onboarding. Five minutes to first shareable page.
- Zero vocabulary tax. "Panel," "page," "chapter" — no "artboard"
  or "document."
- Mobile-friendly — sketches on an iPad.

What they don't need:

- Print DPI. Colour management. Layered PSD exports.

Conversion path to [[Pro tier]] is usually their second or third
ongoing series, when the free-tier caps start to chafe. Not before.

Related: [[Free tier]], [[Cloud library]].
