# Free tier

What a new account gets with no payment:

- 3 active pages ("active" = under 30 days since edit).
- 10 AI layout calls / day.
- No pixel AI calls.
- PNG export only. No print-DPI, no PSD, no PDF.
- Public-share URL available.

Caps are calibrated to make a real tutorial-following beginner feel
unblocked for their first month and then naturally bump against the
ceiling as they get serious.

Don't add more free-tier gates without running the conversion
numbers. The gate is the editor feature, not the drawer.
