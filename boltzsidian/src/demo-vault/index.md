# Sky Notebook

An amateur astronomer's working notebook. An 8" Dobsonian, a pair of 10×50
binoculars, and a suburban yard under [[Bortle scale|Bortle 6]] skies.

## Where I keep things

- Objects I've looked at: [[Sirius]], [[Vega]], [[Betelgeuse]], [[M31 Andromeda]],
  [[M42 Orion Nebula]], [[M45 Pleiades]], [[M13 Hercules Cluster]].
- Shapes in the sky: [[Orion]], [[Ursa Major]], [[Cassiopeia]], [[Lyra]],
  [[Cygnus]], [[Aquila]].
- Ideas I keep coming back to: [[Parallax]], [[Redshift]], [[Precession]],
  [[Supernova]], [[Black hole]].
- People whose shoulders I stand on: [[Galileo]], [[Herschel]], [[Hubble]],
  [[Leavitt]], [[Messier]].

## Anchor projects

- [[Deep sky challenge]] — ninety objects in one calendar year.
- [[Photograph globulars]] — a stack of every Messier globular I can catch.
- [[Catalog my sky]] — my own notebook of what's actually visible from
  this yard on an average Tuesday.
