# M13 first light of the season, 2025-06-02

Warm night, transparency only 5/10 from yard haze but no wind. Set
up late because it's only just getting dark enough by 10:30.

Hercules high overhead. Pointed at [[M13 Hercules Cluster|M13]] and
stepped through the powers — 48×, 80×, 150×, 200×. Best view was
around 150× in the 14mm eyepiece. Outer stars resolved all the way
in to about a third of the radius, then it blurs into the core.

Tried the 6mm for 250× and the seeing was too soft — stars swelled.
Backed off.

A satisfying first look this year. I'll try it again on a colder
night with better transparency.
