# Orion sword, 2025-02-14

Transparency 7/10, seeing 4/10. Moon 15% and setting. Set up the 8" at
9 PM.

[[Orion]] due south and slightly west. Went in on the sword at 80×
with the OIII filter. [[M42 Orion Nebula|M42]] was better than I
remember it being last winter — the bat wings very clear, M43 next to
it as a smaller separate lobe.

Trapezium: four stars split easily at 150×. I tried for E and F and
saw E intermittently; F never came.

After M42 I tried NGC 1999 (the keyhole) — picked it up as a small
smudge but couldn't see the dark notch. Filed for a darker night.

Related: [[Betelgeuse]], [[Rigel]], [[Deep sky challenge]].
