# Andromeda with binoculars, 2024-11-09

Went out with just the 10×50s, no scope. Clear, cold, no Moon.

[[M31 Andromeda]] from the suburban yard in binoculars is still one of
the best sights in the sky for the effort. Found it in the usual way:
the Cassiopeia-W points down and to the left; hop off Beta Cass,
past Mirach in Andromeda, up one more to M31.

Took about 15 seconds from cold. Full extent of the halo was maybe
3° — much bigger than it looks in any photo I'd describe as "full
field."

Tried for M32 and M110. Saw M32 pretty clearly as a small extra
smudge at the south end. M110 was maybe there, maybe averted.

Related: [[Cassiopeia]], [[Bortle scale]], [[Binoculars]].
