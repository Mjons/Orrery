# Binoculars

Nikon Action EX 10×50s. The scope I grab when I don't want to grab
the [[Telescope|scope]]. Bought in 2019, still the best astronomy
purchase I've made per dollar.

**Field of view:** 6.5°. Enough to show [[M31 Andromeda]] with
context, the entire [[M45 Pleiades]] with room to spare, and most
open clusters whole.

**What I use them for:**

- Finding things before setting up the big scope.
- Open clusters and the Milky Way band in [[Cygnus]].
- [[Summer Triangle]] sweeps.
- Lyrid and Perseid meteor watching.
- Casual sessions when rain is threatening.

Tripod adapter in the gear bag — handholding 10× for anything longer
than thirty seconds is shaky even for someone with steady hands.
