# Eyepieces

Current kit, all 1.25" barrel:

- **25 mm Plössl.** 48× in the 8". Finder eyepiece — the one I
  drop in when I'm trying to _locate_ something. Wide field, good
  eye relief.
- **14 mm ES-68.** 86×. Workhorse deep-sky eyepiece. Best view of
  [[M13 Hercules Cluster]] at this power.
- **9 mm Pentax XW.** 133×. Planetary and small planetary nebulae.
  [[M57 Ring Nebula]] is best here.
- **6 mm Baader ortho.** 200×. Seeing-limited most nights. Useful
  on genuinely still evenings.

Want: a longer focal-length wide-field — 30 mm ES-82 or equivalent
— for [[Summer Triangle|wide Milky Way]] sweeps.
