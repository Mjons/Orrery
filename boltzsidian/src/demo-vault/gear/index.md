# Gear

The kit. Nothing fancy — a hobbyist's setup that has been slowly
refined over five years of suburban observing.

- [[Telescope]] — Orion XT8 Dobsonian.
- [[Eyepieces]] — 25 / 14 / 9 / 6 mm, all 1.25".
- [[Binoculars]] — Nikon Action EX 10×50.

**Also in the bag:**

- Telrad finder (paired with a right-angle 8×50 straight-through).
- OIII and UHC filters.
- Red-light headlamp (currently: missing).
- Foam-lined eyepiece tray and bag.
- A thermos with coffee, always.

**Want but don't have:**

- Equatorial tracking platform (a Poncet-style board).
- Dew heater strap.
- 30 mm wide-field eyepiece.
