Search tips

What it searches

  • Looks at every note's title, #tags, and body — you don't pick a field, just type the word.
  • Title and #tag hits rank above body hits, so a note called "Redshift" beats one that just mentions it.

How to type

  • Partial words match — obs finds Obsidian.
  • Typos are forgiven — vauls still finds vault.
  • Multiple words broaden the search — dream sleep matches notes mentioning either.

While you type

  • The universe dims to your hits — only matching stars stay bright. Empty input restores everything.
  • The camera arcs to the top hit as you narrow. Use ↑ ↓ to walk down the list and the camera follows.

Connect mode

  • Start with connect (or link) and a comma-list to batch-link notes: connect cepheid, polaris, parallax finds notes that mention all three and offers to wikilink them to each other.
  • A preview pane opens — uncheck anything that doesn't belong, then Enter applies. The toast that follows holds an Undo for 30 seconds.

Commands

  • Type / as the first character to switch into command mode. Pick a verb, hit Enter to run it.
  • v1 commands: /help, /new [title], /dream, /formation <name>, /clear.

Keys

  • Cmd/Ctrl + K open or close · ↑ ↓ walk hits · Enter open (or apply, in connect mode) · Esc close.
